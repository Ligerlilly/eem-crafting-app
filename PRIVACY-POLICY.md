# Privacy Policy for Eem Crafting Companion

**Last Updated: March 29, 2026**

## Overview

Eem Crafting Companion ("the App") is a companion tool for the Land of Eem tabletop RPG that helps players track crafting components, recipes, and crafting sessions. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We DO NOT Collect

The App does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

- ❌ No personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No usage analytics or tracking
- ❌ No advertising data
- ❌ No data transmitted to external servers
- ❌ No account creation or authentication required

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only** using SQLite database:

- ✅ Component inventory quantities
- ✅ Material counts
- ✅ Crafting session history (recipes crafted, outcomes, materials used)

**Important:** All data is stored exclusively on your device. The App functions completely offline and does not require an internet connection.

## Data Storage and Security

- All app data is stored locally using Expo SQLite
- Data never leaves your device
- No cloud synchronization or backup
- Data is only accessible by the App on your device
- Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all stored data

## Permissions

The App does **not** request or require any device permissions:

- ❌ No camera access
- ❌ No photo library access
- ❌ No location services
- ❌ No contacts access
- ❌ No microphone access
- ❌ No network/internet access required for core functionality

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, analytics, advertising networks, or external APIs.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. As the App does not collect any data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- You can clear all app data through your device's app settings
- Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all associated data
- There is no data to delete from external servers as none is collected

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Summary

**In short:** Eem Crafting Companion is a simple, offline companion app for tabletop RPG players. It stores your crafting inventory locally on your device and collects absolutely no personal information. Your data stays on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.
